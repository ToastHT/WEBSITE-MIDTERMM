About Us

Who We Are

Riot Games was founded in 2006 with the goal of developing, publishing, and supporting the most player-focused games in the world. In 2009, we released our debut title, League of Legends, to worldwide acclaim. League has since gone on to become the most-played PC game in the world and a key driver of the explosive growth of esports.

Our Mission

At Riot Games, we aim to be the most player-focused game company in the world. We believe in delivering amazing experiences to players by fostering creativity, promoting innovation, and empowering players to have their best gaming moments with friends and competitors alike.

Our Games

  • League of Legends: A fast-paced, competitive online game that blends the speed and intensity of a real-time strategy game with role-playing elements.
  • Teamfight Tactics: A round-based strategy game where players draft and position characters to best their opponents in a game of tactics.
  • Valorant: A tactical first-person shooter that blends precision gunplay with unique character abilities.
  • Legends of Runeterra: A strategy card game based on the world of League of Legends.

Our Culture

Riot Games prides itself on creating a culture of collaboration, innovation, and passion for gaming. We believe that players deserve the best, and we strive to achieve excellence in everything we do. We promote an inclusive and supportive environment where every Rioter can thrive, create, and shape the future of gaming.

Our Community

Our players are at the heart of everything we do. From listening to feedback, delivering continuous game updates, to fostering esports competitions and events, we are dedicated to ensuring that our community feels heard, empowered, and excited to be part of the journey.

Join Us

Want to be a part of something special? Riot Games is always looking for talented, passionate people who want to push the boundaries of gaming. Whether you’re a developer, artist, marketer, or player support specialist, we’d love to have you on board.
